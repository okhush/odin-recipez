banana bread.

Desciption

Making this Banana Bread recipe a day ahead and refrigerating overnight allows the bread to taste so much better.

Ingeredients

  • 200g/1.6 cup Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • ½ tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 tsp Cocoa Powder
  • 100g Butter
  • 100g/7 tbsp Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2½ Bananas
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Add the dry ingredients in the bowl through sieve
  3. Beat Butter in another large bowl
  4. Add Sugar into butter and beat again
  5. Add eggs and vanilla essence and mix again into a mush
  6. Mush the banana and add it to the butter mush to make a bigger mush
  7. Add the dry ingredients into the bigger mush to make a larger mush
  8. Pre heat large pot for 10 mins
  9. Pour batter into a pot and place it into the preheated pot
  10. Bake in the preheated pot for 35 minutes. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving
  11. Enjoy!
